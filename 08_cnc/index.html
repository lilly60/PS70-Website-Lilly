So, I went back to square one. I found a more detailed model of a snake head online and turned it into a mold positive. I had to 3D print it becuase model included underhangs.<p> <img src=snake_mod.png width = "500vw"> </img> <p> I then cast plastic into my new mold and painted the snake heads green.<p> <img src=snake1.jpg width = "500vw"> </img> <img src=snake2.jpg width = "500vw"> </img> </div>
